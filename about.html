Just a few words about me

I love coding because it combines two of my biggest values: collaboration and innovation. At UNC’s bootcamp, I excelled at creating useful and interesting front-end experiences, and kept team projects on course by emphasizing communication and affirming teammates.

I am a lifelong learner who has a knack for making teams better, and I will bring a fresh energy to each project in which I participate.
